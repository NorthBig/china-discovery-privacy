Privacy Policy

App: Travel China  |  Last updated: April 5, 2026

Travel China ("we", "our", or "the app") is a travel guide app for exploring destinations in China. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

  • iCloud Account Name — When you create a profile, we read your iCloud given name (with your permission) to pre-fill your display name. You may also set a custom username instead.
  • Profile Photo — If you choose to set an avatar, the photo is uploaded to Apple's CloudKit (iCloud) and stored in your private iCloud container.
  • User Reviews — Reviews and ratings you submit (including any attached photos) are stored in Apple's CloudKit public database and are visible to other users.
  • Location — The app requests your location only when you use the map navigation feature to get directions to a landmark. Location data is not stored or transmitted to any server.
  • Photos / Camera — Camera and photo library access is used solely to let you select or capture images for your profile avatar or reviews. Images are not accessed beyond what you explicitly choose to upload.

2. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, or browsing history.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. AI Travel Planning (DeepSeek & MiniMax)

The app includes an AI-powered itinerary planning feature. When you request a travel plan, the destination name and trip details you enter are sent to the DeepSeek API or the MiniMax API to generate suggestions. Requests may be routed to either provider. No personal identifiers (name, iCloud ID, location) are included in these requests.

4. Data Storage

  • iCloud (CloudKit) — Your profile (name, avatar) and reviews are stored in Apple's CloudKit infrastructure. Apple's privacy policy applies: apple.com/legal/privacy.
  • On-device — Favorites, recently viewed items, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using CoreData and UserDefaults. This data is never transmitted to any server.

5. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the app: Account → Account Settings → Delete Account. Deleting your account removes your profile (name and avatar) from CloudKit. Reviews you have posted remain visible to other users but are no longer linked to your account. Local device data (favorites, recently viewed) is not deleted automatically; to remove it, uninstall the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Travel China is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
